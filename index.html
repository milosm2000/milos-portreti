services and prices

 

I draw realistic portraits with a graphite pencil based on the photo you send me. I use high quality paper and pencils.

 

Once you have decided to order a portrait from me, here is the process:

 

I need a reference photo of the person's face. The photo must be of good quality because the realism of the drawing depends on it.

 

Prices and formats:

 

- A5 format (14.8 cm x 21 cm), one person - 110 EUR - if you order two, the price is 90 per piece !!!!

 

- A4 format (21 cm x 29.7 cm), one person - 140 EUR - when ordering two people, the price is 110 per piece !!!!

 

- A3 format (29.7 cm x 42 cm), one person - 180 EUR - if you order two, the price is 150 per piece !!!!

 

For more drawings we can agree a discount.

 

- Larger paper sizes or drawings by more than one person can also be arranged, in which case the price will be calculated based on your request.

 

The production time is a maximum of 4 weeks.

 

If we make a deal after communicating by whatsapp/email. We need to
meet twice in person.
Once for the prepayment,
(after that the production time starts) and then once for handing over the drawing.
